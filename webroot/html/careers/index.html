A career at Dunder Mifflin is exciting, rewarding, and fun. With branches in five states, Dunder Mifflin is a name people trust. When you work at Dunder Mifflin, you’re carrying on our fine tradition of customer service and quality products at an affordable price. We look for people that are motivated, dedicated, organized, punctual, and responsible. If you feel you have these characteristics, please contact our Human Resources Department.
